FAQ

Common questions

Does Brandly process payments?

No. Brandly is a marketplace for discovery, requests, chat, and reviews. Brands and creators agree on payment terms directly inside their request thread — invoice, bank transfer, or whatever you both choose. We don't hold funds and we take zero commission on your deals.

Can guests contact creators?

No. To send a request you need a Brand account so the creator knows who they're talking to and reviews can be tied to a verified advertiser. Sign up takes under a minute and is free.

When does chat with a creator open?

The private thread opens the moment the creator accepts your request. Until then your brief sits in their inbox and they can either accept it or decline it with a short reason — no random unsolicited DMs.

Which platforms are supported?

TikTok and YouTube are the focus, with Instagram supported for fashion, beauty, and lifestyle creators who use it as their primary channel. Creators link their profile URLs directly — Brandly displays the stats they share.

Who can leave reviews?

Only Brand accounts that completed a real order with the creator. Reviews are public on the creator's profile and tied to the verified advertiser account that posted them — no anonymous reviews, no fake stars.

Can a creator decline a request?

Yes. Every request is an opportunity, not an obligation. Creators can decline with a short reason (budget too low, not relevant, no availability, etc.) and the brand sees it transparently. Accepted requests open the private thread for the actual collaboration.
