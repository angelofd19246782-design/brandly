FAQ

Common questions

How do payments work?

Brandly is a marketplace, not a payment processor. Brands and creators agree on payment terms directly inside their request thread — invoice, bank transfer, or whatever you both agree on. We don't hold funds and we don't take commission.

What does it cost to use Brandly?

Creators are free, forever. Brands start free with 50 creator searches and 10 active threads per month — no card needed. Pro is $49/month for unlimited usage. Business adds team seats for agencies.

Can a creator decline a request?

Yes — every request is an opportunity, not an obligation. Creators can decline with a short reason, and the brand sees it transparently. Accepted requests open a private thread for the actual conversation.

Who can leave reviews?

Only brands who have an actual conversation with a creator can rate them. Reviews are public on the creator's profile and tied to the verified advertiser account that posted them — no anonymous reviews, no fake stars.

Does Brandly verify creator audiences?

Creators add their own platform stats — followers, niches, locations. Brandly does not run audience-quality analysis. Brands rely on past reviews from other brands and the creator's public profile to make their decision.

Can I run a campaign with multiple creators?

Yes. Each request is its own thread, but Pro and Business plans give you a campaign view across all your active creator deals so you can track them together.
