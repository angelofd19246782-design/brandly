Frequently asked

Common questions

Is there a free plan?

Yes. Our Starter plan is free forever and includes 50 creator searches per month, basic filters, and up to 10 active conversations — no credit card required.

Do I pay per message or per creator?

No usage charges. Every plan includes a generous message allowance and unlimited profile views. You only pay the flat monthly fee.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel with one click from your account settings. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Your data stays available for 90 days after cancellation.

Is there a discount for annual billing?

Yes — pay annually and save 20% on any paid plan. Annual subscribers also get extended support and early access to new features.

Do you offer custom enterprise plans?

Yes. For agencies managing 10+ brands or teams needing SSO, audit logs, and dedicated infrastructure, talk to our sales team for a custom plan.
